## Print Specifications

- **Nozzle Temperature:** 225°C
- **Bed Temperature:** 70°C
- **Layer Height:** 0.12 mm
- **Filament Length:** 0.23 m (227 mm)

## Notes

- This print is optimized for the **Ender-3 V2** with **BLTouch**.
- Uses off-print purge to prevent nozzle blobs on first layer.
- Compatible with Cura slicer.
- Test on a small print first before large jobs.
