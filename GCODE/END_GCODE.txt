; Ender 3 V2 - Production End G-Code
; Status: PRODUCTION BASELINE (as of 2025-11-09)
; Purpose: Final retract, park, and warm hold for quick reprints
;
; Features:
;   • 4mm retraction at end (prevents oozing)
;   • 10mm Z-lift (clear print safely)
;   • Nozzle parks at front-left (easy access)
;   • 5-minute warm hold (180°C nozzle, 70°C bed)
;   • Ready for quick reprint or cool shutdown
;
; To use: Paste into Cura → Printer Settings → Machine Settings → End G-Code

; --- RETRACT & LIFT ---
G91 ; Relative positioning
G1 E-2 F2700 ; Retract filament 2mm quickly
G1 E-2 Z0.2 F2400 ; Retract another 2mm and raise Z 0.2mm
G1 Z10 F3000 ; Raise Z axis 10mm total (clear print)

G90 ; Switch back to Absolute positioning

; --- PARK & COOL SLIGHTLY ---
G1 X0 Y{machine_depth} ; Park nozzle at front-left (easy access)
M106 S0 ; Turn-off cooling fan

; --- WARM HOLD PHASE (for quick reprints) ---
M104 S180 ; Nozzle to 180°C (warm standby, 60s to reach 220°C)
M140 S70 ; Bed to 70°C (close to material temps)

G4 P300000 ; **DWELL:** Pause 5 minutes (300,000ms) - time to assess print

; --- FINAL SHUTDOWN ---
M104 S0 ; Turn-off hotend
M140 S0 ; Turn-off bed

M84 X Y E ; Disable X, Y, E steppers (Z remains locked to hold position)
